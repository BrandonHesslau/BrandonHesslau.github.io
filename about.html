About Me

Hi there!

My name is Brandon Hesslau, and I'm a passionate software engineer with a focus on game development.

I first fell in love with programming and creating games back in high school, and I've been pursuing this passion ever since. I studied computer science and real-time game systems at DePaul University, and I'm proud to have graduated in 2021. With a strong desire to turn my passion into a profession, I co-founded an indie game studio during my last year of college. Together, we successfully developed and released a successful game called Buddy Simulator 1984.

I have experience working with a variety of programming languages such as C++, C#, Python, Java, and Lua, and I'm proficient in Unity 3D and Unreal Engine, gameplay systems, game engine development, AI engineering, and graphics programming. In my free time, I'm always working on new projects, whether it's developing game prototypes, creating tools to help other developers, or exploring experimental ideas that pique my curiosity. I strongly believe that successful developers need to have both technical and creative mindsets, and I'm dedicated to further exploring and expanding on these practices in my personal projects. I love working closely with others to solve problems and create exciting new experiences. So, if you're looking for a skilled and passionate developer for your next project, please feel free to reach out!
